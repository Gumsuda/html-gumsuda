Who We are

On 07 July 2024 the organization “Gumsuda” was established at University Institute Of Technology The University of Burdwan, Golapbag (North), Burdwan-713104. West Bengal our aims to help people who have lost their loved ones due to various reasons, such as natural disasters, conflicts, migration, or human trafficking. We believe that everyone deserves to know the whereabouts and well-being of their family members and friends, and we are committed to making this possible.

How We Work

We work in collaboration with local ashrams and NGOs that provide shelter, care, and support to the people who are separated from their families. We use our innovative technology and network to locate and contact the missing persons, and facilitate their reunion with their loved ones. We also provide counseling and legal assistance to the people who need it.

Services We Offer

We offer two type of services first is finding people and another one is gps tracking band.

Finding people

We find people who lost their loved ones due to various reasons, such as natural disasters, conflicts, migration, or human trafficking. through collaboration with local ashrams and NGOs that provide shelter
